About us

SUS ENERGY is a Freetown-based solar installation and electrical works company committed to delivering reliable, affordable, and clean energy solutions across Sierra Leone. We serve residential, commercial, and institutional clients by providing professionally installed solar systems, complete electrical wiring services, and ongoing maintenance support.

Our Services

  1. Solar Panel Installation — Design, supply, and installation of solar PV systems for homes, offices, schools, and businesses; and consulting, engineering, and associated services.
  2. Electrical Wiring — Complete building wiring, installation, connection, earthing, and commissioning of electrical equipment, systems, and controls.
  3. Maintenance & Repair — Solar panel installation, sales, designing, and services for homes, offices, and schools, businesses and customers' residences.
