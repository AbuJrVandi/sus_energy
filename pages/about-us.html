About Us

LIGHT UP. POWER UP. SUS ENERGY

SUS ENERGY is a Freetown-based solar installation and electrical works company committed to delivering reliable, affordable, and clean energy solutions across Sierra Leone. We serve residential, commercial, and institutional clients by providing professionally installed solar systems, complete electrical wiring services, and ongoing maintenance support.

Founded on the belief that access to stable power is essential to economic growth, SUS ENERGY brings together certified electricians and skilled installation technicians to deliver projects safely and to the highest standard. We are a business-owner-led company built on trust, professionalism, and results.

Our Vision

To be Sierra Leone's most trusted energy partner, powering homes, businesses, and institutions with reliable solar and electrical solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver clean, affordable energy access across Sierra Leone through expert installation, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering customer service.

Our Values

Integrity: We do what we say and stand behind our work.
Excellence: Every installation meets the highest standard of quality and safety.
Community: We believe reliable power transforms lives and drives growth.

Why Choose SUS Energy?

Powering Your World with Solar & Electrical Excellence

  • Elegance & Excellence — We deliver premium solar installations with professional craftsmanship.
  • Certified Professionals — Our electricians and installation technicians are fully qualified and experienced.
  • End-to-End Service — From design and permitting to installation and maintenance, we handle it all.
  • Reliable Energy — Affordable, clean energy solutions that reduce dependence on the grid.
